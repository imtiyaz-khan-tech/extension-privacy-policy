Privacy Policy
One of our main priorities is the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by our chrome extensions and how we use it.
Information we collect!

  • Reading salesforce session to perform read actions.
  • Using tooling patch for some updation like when user click on edit record detail and custom labels for now.
